Our Services

24 hours support

We facilitate our patients remaining in their own homes with dignity, independence and control over their lives. Regardless of the level of support needed, a member of the Health Care team is available 24/7

Emergency Services

We provide the gateway to the hospital, patients with acutely ill and injured, relative emotionally charged enter the emergency department at any hour of the day or night, expecting the immediate treatment and the solace.

Ambulance Services

We provide 24 hours ambulance support, these ambulances are well equipped with oxygen tank, strecher, wheelchair and first
aid box that are necessary to provide the stability and relief to patients.

Our Specialists

Our specialists are expertise in their work having more than 5 years of experience.

Child Specialist

Our child specialists manages the physical, behavioral, and mental care for children from birth until age 18. They are trained to diagnose and treat a broad range of childhood illnesses, from minor health problems to serious diseases.

Neurologist

Our neurologists are expert in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the brain and nervous system. They also treat illnesses such as neuro-developmental disorders, learning disabilities, and other central nervous system-related conditions

Cardiologist

Our cardiologists are specialized in diagnosing and treating diseases or conditions of the heart and blood vessels of the cardiovascular system. The treats heart attacks, heart failure, heart valve disease, arrhythmia, and high blood pressure.
